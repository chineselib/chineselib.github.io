Book Donations

We gladly accept donations of Chinese-language children’s books (ages 0–12).
Donated books help support:
  • Sprout Pack, our book gifting program
  • Community sharing shelves
  • Reading and storytelling activities
If you are considering a bulk donation or have specific titles, please contact us in advance.
📩 Contact us: admin@chineselib.org
Individual Donations
If you’d like to support Chinese reading in a more ongoing way, here are a few options:
  • Create a personal mini library on our Little Forest platform and share books with nearby families
  • Donate books to a local Chinese Library for Children branch
  • Pass books directly to families who are building home libraries in your community

We believe books are most meaningful when they circulate among families and are read again and again.

Corporate Donations & Partnerships
We welcome corporate and organizational support in a variety of ways, including:
  • Sponsor Sprout Pack book gifting for a season or a full year
  • Support reading access in a specific city or community
  • Donate excess or backlist inventory (publishers and distributors)
  • Contribute original works (authors and creators)

We are also happy to work with partners to design long-term, sustainable reading support initiatives.
📩 Partnership inquiries: admin@chineselib.org
