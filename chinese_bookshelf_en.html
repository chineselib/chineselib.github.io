Chinese Shelf —— U.S. Public Library Collection Optimization Program

Connecting premium publishing resources with public library systems to share contemporary Chinese stories with the world.

  1. Curated Excellence: Selection of Premium Works
      2. Domestic Synergy:We establish deep partnerships with leading Chinese publishers and original picture book authors to build a dynamic "Overseas Export Repository."
      Professional Review: We curate works that resonate with the cognitive development of second-generation Chinese-American children while maintaining contemporary aesthetic and universal values, ensuring every book in the collection is a masterpiece.
      Annual Selection: Release the "CLC Annual Recommended List" every year. We ensure library collections remain current and vibrant, allowing international readers to access the latest high-quality Chinese literature.
  2. Systematic Institutional Support
      3. Bibliographic Database: Provide standardized English descriptions and metadata for all selected titles.
      National Cataloging Integration: Synchronize data with global library networks (e.g., OCLC), enabling seamless "one-click" integration into local library management systems.
  3. Channel Integration & Global Promotion
      4. Procurement Solutions: Assist libraries in connecting with approved vendors that meet official requirements, simplifying the specialized acquisition process.
      Global Visibility: Enhance the presence of original Chinese children’s literature in mainstream public spaces, fostering authentic cultural integration within grassroots communities.
Partners & Values
  • For Chinese Publishers & Authors: We are your most strategic gateway to the U.S. public library system.
  • For U.S. Public Libraries: We are your specialized consultants for Chinese collection development.
  • For the Chinese-American Community:We are your dedicated advocates for cultural equity.
