Building a Chinese Reading Ecosystem

We seek to foster a love of reading, support bilingual and heritage language development, and create inclusive opportunities for families to engage with Chinese literature and culture.
Through three interconnected pillars — Book Gifting (Opening the Door to Reading), Book Sharing (Sustaining Everyday Reading), and Reading Together (Deepening Engagement and Connection) — we cultivate a living ecosystem that grows alongside children on their Chinese reading journey.
