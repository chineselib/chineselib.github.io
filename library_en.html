Chinese Library Corners

We partner with public libraries across the United States to build free, high-quality Chinese children’s book collections for local families.

Each Library Corner includes carefully curated picture books, early readers, and family-friendly Chinese reading materials. All books are selected based on age appropriateness, print quality, simplified Chinese, and cultural relevance.

Our goal is to make Chinese reading resources easier to access for families in all communities—whether you are maintaining the heritage language or newly exploring it.
