Little Forest — Where Families Share Language and Culture

One library, one tree. One neighborhood, one growing forest.

Little Forest is a community-powered platform where families share Chinese-language children’s books with nearby households.

Along the way, children experience Chinese language and culture through stories, books, and simple, real-life connections within their neighborhood.

Enter Little Forest App
Why Families Use Little Forest
  • To introduce Chinese language without formal classes
  • To access better books without high costs
  • To connect with other families who value language and culture
  • To make bilingual exposure part of everyday life, not another obligation
Designed with Privacy and Trust in Mind
  • Little Forest is built for families. You don’t need to share your exact address or enable precise location tracking to participate.
  • Families discover nearby libraries by ZIP code and distance range, while hosts can choose how much location detail to share.
  • All communication happens within the platform, allowing families to connect, ask questions, and coordinate book sharing comfortably and safely.
