2026, Spring Sprout Pack, Session No.1

《灶王爷》——献给新年的第一份阅读礼物

《灶王爷》是一部充满年味与温情的中国传统节日绘本。创作者熊亮以细腻的画风、朴拙的线条和东方色彩，重新讲述了灶王爷上天言好事、下界保平安的古老民间故事。书中既有关于春节的传统习俗，也有孩子能够立刻感受到的家庭亲情与温暖。

作为我们“萌芽包”第一期的新年绘本，《灶王爷》希望陪伴孩子在春节前后，认识家中厨房的守护神，感受新年的仪式感，体验善良、希望与团圆的意义。

这是一本适合在年夜饭前、贴春联时、或睡前共读的绘本，让新年的第一个故事，为孩子带来属于自己的好运与祝福。

Sprout Pack — Where Language and Culture Take Root

One season. One book. One gentle step into Chinese language and culture.


Sprout Pack Sprout Pack is a quarterly, free book-gifting program for children ages 2–5.
Each season, families receive one thoughtfully selected Chinese-language picture book, delivered straight to their mailbox.

Sign Up Now

Why Families Choose Sprout Pack

  • 🌏 Gentle exposure to Chinese language through real stories
  • 🧧 Cultural context beyond translation
  • 🏡 Easy to share at home—even if parents don’t speak Chinese
  • 📦 One book each season, building familiarity over time

Small Books. Lasting Connections.

Shipping Schedule
Sprout Pack is mailed four times a year, with deliveries scheduled for:

  • Early February
  • Early May
  • Early September (shifted later due to the summer break)
  • Early November

How to Join
Registration opens about two months before each mailing.
Exact dates will be announced on our website—feel free to check back for the latest updates.
To make sure you don’t miss a signup window, we recommend following our community channels (Red Note and Wechat) for reminders and announcements.
