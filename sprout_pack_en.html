《伴我长大经典童话》

这些跨越了几十年的故事，
曾照亮过我们的双眼，
现在，轮到我们讲给孩子听了。

在异国他乡的灯下，这些故事不只是语言的学习，更是血脉里的共鸣。
孩子读到了勇敢与智慧，而我们，在阅读中重逢了那个小时候的自己。

这是一份跨越山海的礼物，也是两代人之间最温柔的共同语言。

Why Families Choose Sprout Pack

  • 🌏 Gentle exposure to Chinese language through real stories
  • 🧧 Cultural context beyond translation
  • 🏡 Easy to share at home—even if parents don’t speak Chinese
  • 📦 One book each season, building familiarity over time

Small Books. Lasting Connections.

Shipping Schedule
Sprout Pack is mailed four times a year, with deliveries scheduled for:

  • Early February
  • Early May
  • Early September (shifted later due to the summer break)
  • Early November

How to Join
Registration opens about one months before each mailing.
Exact dates will be announced on our website—feel free to check back for the latest updates.
To make sure you don’t miss a signup window, we recommend following our community channels (Red Note and Wechat) for reminders and announcements.
